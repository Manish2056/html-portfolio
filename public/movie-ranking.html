The Best Movies According to Manish

My favourite movies and animes of all-time.

One Piece

This anime is one of the best anime with many episodes and the best adventure story you can get.
Do check this anime out if you are one of the people who loves adventure genre.

Van Helsing

You are in for a treat if you are the one who likes to watch movies based on mythology and fantasy.
Do watch this movie if you want to watch some action between a Werewolf and a Vampire.

Indiana Jones

I recommend this movie and its different for the people who loves to watch mystery and treasure hunting sort of movies.
Do watch the different parts of this movie if you wanna see Professor Jones uncovering the secrets of different civilizations packed with action.
